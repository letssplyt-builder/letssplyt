How it works

From QR code to settled — in one evening

An event-based flow designed for real dinners, not running ledgers.

  1. 1

    Create & show QR

    The creator starts an event and displays a QR code. No spreadsheets, no awkward math at the table.

  2. 2

    Everyone joins live

    App members scan and join instantly. Guests open a browser, verify their phone, and appear in real time.

  3. 3

    Scan the receipt

    AI reads items, tax, and tip. Assign dishes by voice or tap. Math is exact — penny-perfect splits.

  4. 4

    Pay & confirm

    Personalised messages with split images go out via SMS. People pay via Venmo, PayPal, or their favourite app.
